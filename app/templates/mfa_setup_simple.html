WHAT IS MFA?

Multi-Factor Authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account by requiring a time-based code from your authenticator app in addition to your password.

HOW TO ENABLE MFA:

MFA is managed through your AWS Cognito account settings.

  1. Click the button below to go to MFA settings
  2. Follow Cognito's instructions to set up MFA
  3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
  4. Enter the verification code to confirm
  5. MFA will be required on your next login
